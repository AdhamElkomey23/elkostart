About Elkostart

We're Not Just Designers. We're Growth Partners.

Every pixel we place, every line of code we write, every strategy we craft — it's all designed with one goal: helping your business thrive online.

Our Story

From Frustration to Foundation

Elkostart was born from a simple frustration: watching brilliant businesses struggle with websites that didn't reflect their true value. We saw companies with incredible products, passionate founders, and loyal customers — all held back by outdated, underperforming websites.

In 2019, we set out to change that. We built a team that combines creative vision with technical excellence and, most importantly, a deep understanding of what makes businesses grow. We don't just make things look pretty — we make them work.

Today, we've helped over 150 businesses transform their online presence into their most powerful growth engine. But we're still driven by the same belief that started it all: every business deserves a website that works as hard as they do.

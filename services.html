FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take 4-12 weeks depending on scope and complexity. We'll give you a detailed timeline during our discovery call, and we stick to our deadlines.

Do you work with clients outside your timezone?

Absolutely. We have clients worldwide and are experienced in async communication. We'll find meeting times that work for both of us.

What if I'm not happy with the design?

Every project includes multiple revision rounds. We work collaboratively and gather feedback throughout, so there are no surprises. We're not done until you're thrilled.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We typically split payments into milestones — a deposit to start, payment at design approval, and final payment at launch. We can discuss options that work for your budget.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

Yes. We build most sites on user-friendly CMS platforms and provide training so you can make basic updates yourself. For bigger changes, we're always here to help.

What happens after the website launches?

We don't disappear. Every project includes post-launch support, and we offer ongoing maintenance plans for businesses that want continued peace of mind.

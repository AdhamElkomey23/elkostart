About This Design

This demo project showcases a modern approach to automotive dealership website design. The focus is on creating a professional, trustworthy feel that appeals to car buyers while providing an intuitive browsing and booking experience.

Key Design Features

  • Dynamic vehicle inventory display with high-quality image galleries
  • Advanced filtering by make, model, year, price, and features
  • Online test drive scheduling and appointment booking
  • Trade-in valuation calculator integrated into the platform
  • Financing options overview and loan calculator
  • Mobile-optimized layouts for on-the-go browsing
